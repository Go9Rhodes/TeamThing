Here are the keywords I support today:
  • #ADD# - Adds a new thing to TeamThing board for you today
  • #REMOVE# - Removes a thing already on the board today
  • #UNDO# - Unremoves a thing and puts it back on today's board
  • #DONE# - Marks a thing on today's board as done (yay!)
  • #DELAY# - Moves a thing on today's board to tomorrow
To use a keyword, it needs to be the first word on a new line (followed by the thing text). And for all commands other than #ADD#, the thing text needs to match the original. I hate to be so strict, but it makes my life easier. I'll try to understand more in the future.
